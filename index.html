Odysseus

Who is Odysseus?

  • Hero of Homer's epic poem the Odyssey
  • Appears as the man best suited to cope with crises in personal relations among the Greeks in the story Iliad

What makes Odysseus important?

  • Creates the idea of the Trojan Horse, which allowed the Greeks to end the 10 year long Trojan War
  • Plays a leading part in achieving the reconciliation between Agamemnon and Achilles
